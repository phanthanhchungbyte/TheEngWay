<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">This time the young man walked into a long narrow valley. He saw a bear and a deer at a spring. They were drinking water from a small pool. The young man was thirsty. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">He was about to shout at the bear and the deer and ask for water when something stopped him. &ldquo;Is this strange? - A bear and a deer drinking together?&rdquo;, he thought. After a while, he decided to speak. &ldquo;May I take a drink?&rdquo; he asked. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich" style="background-color: white; cursor: default;"> The bear and the deer looked up and said &ldquo;There is plenty of water. There is no scarcity. The water belongs to us all.&rdquo; After having drunk, the young man sat down to rest and the bear and the deer came to rest with him. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich" style="background-color: white; cursor: default;">The young man asked if they were friends. &ldquo;No, we are not friends but we learn from each other&rdquo;, said the bear. And the deer continued &ldquo;One day the bear and I came to the spring at the same time. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">We were both thirsty and had a violent fight. After the fight both of us were hurting and bloody. No one was happy.&rdquo; The bear continued &ldquo;We realized that it seemed better not to fight; and to share the water of the spring&rdquo;. </span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">The young man smiled and laid on the grass for a rest. And the wind whispered into his ears: &ldquo;It is wrong to fight when both want the same thing and there is enough for both&rdquo;. The man rested for a while and then left the deer and the bear sleeping in the warm sunlight. He had received his meaningful lesson of life. </span></p>
