<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">Today we will continue with the story of the young man who wanted to find the meaning of life. After the experience he gained from the butterfly&rsquo;s story, what other lessons will he learn? </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich" style="background-color: white; cursor: default;">When he was on his journey, it began to rain. As the rain got heavier and heavier, he had to find a cave for shelter. In the small cave he watched the harsh, cold wind blowing outside and the lightening lit the hillside with its brightness. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich" style="background-color: white; cursor: default;">Nearby there was a great, solid oak tree and also a small willow tree fighting with the violent wind. The oak tree spoke proudly &ldquo;I&rsquo;m strong, and brave. I've never been afraid of anyone or anything.&rdquo; Suddenly, the violent wind blew, and the oak tree was broken in only a few minutes. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">The young man heard the fallen oak say: &ldquo;I&rsquo;m strong, rigid, solid and now broken.&rdquo; The willow said, &ldquo;I&rsquo;m flexible. I&rsquo;m a toy to the wind. I bend.&rdquo; As the willow tree bend and swayed, bowing low to the ground, it survived. </span></p>
<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">When the storm became quiet, the young man got out of the cave. He has just got his second lesson of his trip: &ldquo;Learn to bend, and move like the willow in the current; for then you can watch great things and learn from them without breaking.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p class="m_vd_text"><span class="ta123_dich">Bản dịch</span></p>

<p>Khi anh đang tr&ecirc;n h&agrave;nh tr&igrave;nh, trời bắt đầu mưa. Khi mưa ng&agrave;y c&agrave;ng nặng hạt, anh phải t&igrave;m một c&aacute;i hang để tr&uacute; ẩn. Trong hang động nhỏ, anh nh&igrave;n cơn gi&oacute; lạnh khắc nghiệt thổi b&ecirc;n ngo&agrave;i v&agrave; &aacute;nh chớp chiếu s&aacute;ng sườn đồi.</p>
<p>Gần đ&oacute; c&oacute; một c&acirc;y sồi to lớn chắc nịch v&agrave; cũng c&oacute; một c&acirc;y liễu nhỏ đang chống chọi với những cơn gi&oacute; dữ dội. C&acirc;y sồi tự h&agrave;o n&oacute;i: &ldquo;T&ocirc;i mạnh mẽ v&agrave; dũng cảm. T&ocirc;i chưa bao giờ sợ bất cứ ai hay bất cứ điều g&igrave;.&rdquo; Đột nhi&ecirc;n, một cơn gi&oacute; dữ dội thổi qua v&agrave; c&acirc;y sồi bị g&atilde;y chỉ trong v&agrave;i ph&uacute;t.</p>
<p>Ch&agrave;ng trai nghe c&acirc;y sồi đổ n&oacute;i: &ldquo;T&ocirc;i mạnh mẽ, cứng nhắc, rắn chắc v&agrave; giờ đ&atilde; g&atilde;y vụn&rdquo;. C&acirc;y liễu n&oacute;i: &ldquo;T&ocirc;i linh hoạt. T&ocirc;i l&agrave; một m&oacute;n đồ chơi của gi&oacute;. T&ocirc;i uốn cong.&rdquo; Khi c&acirc;y liễu uốn cong v&agrave; lắc lư, c&uacute;i thấp xuống đất, n&oacute; vẫn sống s&oacute;t.</p>
<p>Khi cơn b&atilde;o y&ecirc;n lặng, ch&agrave;ng trai ra khỏi hang.
