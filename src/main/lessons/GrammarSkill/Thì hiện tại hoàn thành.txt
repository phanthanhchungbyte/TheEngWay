<h2><span style="color: #3598db; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">1. C&aacute;ch d&ugrave;ng</span></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">H&agrave;nh động xảy ra trong qu&aacute; khứ, k&eacute;o d&agrave;i đến hiện tại</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">H&agrave;nh động xảy ra nhiều lần trong qu&aacute; khứ, vẫn k&eacute;o d&agrave;i đến hiện tại</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
</div>
<h2><span style="color: #3598db; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">2. Cấu tr&uacute;c</span></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u khẳng định: <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>S + have/has + V3</strong></span></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u phủ định: <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>S + have/has + not + V3</strong></span></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u nghi vấn: <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Have/Has + S + V3?</strong></span></span></li>
</ul>
<div>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #3598db; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">3. Dấu hiệu nhận biết</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Just</strong>&nbsp;=&nbsp;<strong>Recently</strong>&nbsp;=&nbsp;<strong>Lately</strong></span>: gần đ&acirc;y, vừa mới</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Already</strong></span>: rồi</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Before</strong></span>: trước đ&acirc;y</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Never</strong></span>: chưa bao giờ</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Ever</strong></span>: từng</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Yet</strong></span>: chưa</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>For + khoảng thời gian</strong></span>: for 4 years, for a long time, for a month&hellip;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Since+ mốc thời gian cụ thể</strong></span>: since 2021, since January&hellip;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>The first/ second&hellip; time</strong></span>: lần đầu ti&ecirc;n, lần thứ hai&hellip;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>So far =&nbsp;Until now =&nbsp;Up to now =&nbsp;Up to the present</strong></span>: cho đến b&acirc;y giờ</span></li>
</ul>
