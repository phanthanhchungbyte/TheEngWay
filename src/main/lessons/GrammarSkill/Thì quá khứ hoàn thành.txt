<h2><span style="color: #3598db; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">2. Cấu tr&uacute;c</span></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span id="Cau_truc_the_khang_dinh" style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>a) Cấu tr&uacute;c thể khẳng định</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Cấu tr&uacute;c: <span style="color: #e03e2d;">S + had + V3/ed</span></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span id="Cau_truc_the_phu_dinh" style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>b) Cấu tr&uacute;c thể phủ định</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Cấu tr&uacute;c: <span style="color: #e03e2d;">S + had + not + V3/ed</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Viết tắt: Had not = hadn&rsquo;t</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span id="Cau_truc_the_nghi_van" style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>c) Cấu tr&uacute;c thể nghi vấn</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Cấu tr&uacute;c Yes/No question: <span style="color: #e03e2d;">Had (not) + S + V3/ed + O?</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Cấu tr&uacute;c Wh- question: <span style="color: #e03e2d;">Wh- + had + S + V3/ed + O?</span></strong></span></p>
