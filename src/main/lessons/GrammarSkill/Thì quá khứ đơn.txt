<h2><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #3598db;">2. Cấu tr&uacute;c</span></span></h2>
<div>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u khẳng định: <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>S + V-ed + &hellip;</strong></span></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u phủ định: <strong><span style="color: #e03e2d;">S + did + not + V + &hellip;</span></strong></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u nghi vấn: <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>Did + S + V+ &hellip;?&nbsp;</strong></span></span></li>
</ul>

<h2><span style="color: #3598db; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">3. Dấu hiệu nhận biết</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong><span style="color: #e03e2d;">Yesterday, ago, last (week, year, month), in the past, the day before</span></strong>, v&agrave; những khoảng thời gian đ&atilde; qua trong ng&agrave;y (<span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>today, this morning, this afternoon</strong></span>).</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Sau <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>as if, it&rsquo;s time</strong></span> (đ&atilde; đến l&uacute;c), <strong><span style="color: #e03e2d;">as though</span></strong> (như thể l&agrave;), <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>if only</strong></span>, <span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong>wish</strong></span> (ước g&igrave;), <strong><span style="color: #e03e2d;">would sooner/rather</span></strong> (th&iacute;ch hơn).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: bold; color: #e03e2d; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #e03e2d; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>It&rsquo;s + (high) time + S + Ved</strong></span></li>
<li style="color: #e03e2d; font-weight: bold; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong><span style="color: #e03e2d;">It + is + khoảng thời gian + since + th&igrave; qu&aacute; khứ</span></strong></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">C&acirc;u điều kiện loại 2,&hellip;</span></li>
</ul>

<h2><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #3598db;">4. V&iacute; dụ</span></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">I watched that movie yesterday.&nbsp;<em>(T&ocirc;i đ&atilde; xem phim ấy v&agrave;o h&ocirc;m qua).</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">When the bell rang, the students left the classroom.&nbsp;<em>(Khi tiếng chu&ocirc;ng reo, c&aacute;c học sinh đ&atilde; rời khỏi lớp học.)</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Before it started raining, we went for a walk in the park.&nbsp;<em>(Trước khi trời mưa, ch&uacute;ng t&ocirc;i đ&atilde; đi dạo trong c&ocirc;ng vi&ecirc;n.)</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">When she was a student, she called her family every day.&nbsp;<em>(Khi c&ograve;n l&agrave; sinh vi&ecirc;n, c&ocirc; ấy đ&atilde; gọi cho gia đ&igrave;nh mỗi ng&agrave;y).</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">When I was a child, I used to play hide-and-seek with my friends.<em>&nbsp;(Hồi c&ograve;n nhỏ, t&ocirc;i thường xuy&ecirc;n chơi trốn t&igrave;m với c&aacute;c bạn.)</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">I used to enjoy reading detective novels.&nbsp;<em>(Trước kia, t&ocirc;i th&iacute;ch đọc s&aacute;ch trinh th&aacute;m.)</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">To prepare for the exam, I studied hard, reviewed carefully, and got a high score. (Để chuẩn bị cho kỳ thi, t&ocirc;i đ&atilde; học tập chăm chỉ, &ocirc;n luyện kỹ lưỡng, v&agrave; thi đạt điểm cao.)</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">She went out, locked the door carefully and walked towards the library.<em>&nbsp;(C&ocirc; ấy ra ngo&agrave;i, đ&oacute;ng cửa cẩn thận v&agrave; bước về hướng thư viện).</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">On Sunday mornings, my family used to wake up early, go to the market, cook lunch, eat lunch, and go to the park.&nbsp;<em>(V&agrave;o buổi s&aacute;ng chủ nhật, gia đ&igrave;nh t&ocirc;i thường dậy sớm, đi chợ, nấu ăn, ăn trưa, v&agrave; đi chơi c&ocirc;ng vi&ecirc;n.)</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Henry was riding his bike when it rained.&nbsp;<em>(Khi Henry đang l&aacute;i xe đạp th&igrave; trời mưa).</em></span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">While I was reading, the phone rang suddenly.<em> (Khi t&ocirc;i đang đọc s&aacute;ch, chợt tiếng chu&ocirc;ng điện thoại reo.)</em></span></li>
</ul>
